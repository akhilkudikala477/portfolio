About Me

Engineering Quality into Every Release

I'm a QA Automation Engineer passionate about building test infrastructure that scales. Over 2+ years, I've worked across the full spectrum of quality engineering — designing frameworks from scratch, writing automation that actually sticks, and making sure nothing slips through to production.
